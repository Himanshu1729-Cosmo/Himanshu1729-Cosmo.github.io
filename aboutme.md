### Himanshu Chaudhary

Himanshu Chaudhary is a PhD researcher in cosmology at Babeș-Bolyai University, Cluj-Napoca, Romania. His work focuses on understanding the nature of dark energy beyond the standard $\Lambda$CDM model. His current research explores constraints on the behaviour of dark energy using recent observational data, including DESI, as well as the impact of strong progenitor age bias in supernova cosmology.

You can see more about his [**research**](research.md) or [**download CV**](https://www.dropbox.com/s/o1vxmjvus3y4ewj/CV_eng.pdf?dl=0). For collaborations, projects or job opportunities, please [**send an e-mail**](mailto:himanshuch1729@gmail.com).
